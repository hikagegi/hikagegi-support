HIKAGEGI is a functional clothing project designed to create personal space in modern environments.

It combines minimal design, light control, and freedom of movement — not as fashion, but as a tool for how you live.

I’ve lived in 8 countries, and often felt how difficult it is to stay yourself in different environments.

This project comes from that experience.

I am currently developing this product together with a manufacturing partner in Vietnam.

From fabric selection to structure and production, everything is being built step by step.

I am also building the sales infrastructure myself — learning web development independently to create and run the online store.

This is an independent project.

Your support directly contributes to production, refinement, and scaling.

Your support also helps fund sampling, the initial 50-piece production, and global shipping via EMS.

This is not just a product or a store.

It is an effort to help people live boldly and independently, on their own terms.
