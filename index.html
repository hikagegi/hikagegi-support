HIKAGEGI is a functional clothing project designed to help people define sovereignty in modern environments. It combines minimal design, light control, and freedom of movement — not as fashion, but as a tool to design how you live.

I’ve lived in 8 countries, and often felt how difficult it is to stay yourself in different environments. This project comes from that experience.

I am currently developing this product together with a manufacturing partner in Vietnam. From fabric selection to structure and production, everything is being built step by step.

I am also building the sales infrastructure myself — learning web development independently to create and run the online store.

This is an independent project. Your support directly contributes to production, refinement, and scaling, including sampling, the initial 50-piece production, and global shipping.

This project does not end with a product. The online store is only the beginning. The goal is to build tools — through products and sessions — to help people live boldly and independently.

You are not just supporting a product. You are supporting the creation of a new way of living.
